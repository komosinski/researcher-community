The "researcher status" and endorsement

Some users are automatically endrosed based on their institutional email address.

To get the researcher status, you can ask other endorsed users (via the button in their profile pages) to endorse you. Alternatively, you can send a message to our staff to have your institution included or your particular account endorsed.
